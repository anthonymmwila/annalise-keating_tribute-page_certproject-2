About Professor Keating

Professor Annalise Keating, Esq. (born 'Anna Mae Harkness') was born in 1967, to Ophelia & Mac Harkness in Memphis, Tennessee and grew up a brilliant lady there. She worked with Memphis Legal Aid during her undergraduate degree at the University of Tennessee, after which she attended the Harvard Law School.

Miss Keating is a tenacious, charismatic law professor and defense attorney... A redoubtable, multifaceted legal powerhouse with an unwavering dedication to justice. Annalise is known for her formidable intelligence, cunning legal tactics, and unwavering determination to win her cases at any cost. She is commanding and elegant, with a strong presence that demands attention. Her demeanor is typically confident and assertive, befitting her status as a top-tier defense attorney. Annalise has her own struggles with personal demons, and deep-seated vulnerabilities, including a troubled and traumatic past that still haunts her. Despite all of it, and the death of her husband, which shook her and also brought with it many seemingly unbearable problems, Annalise remains committed to the cause of making other people's lives brighter and getting justice for the convicts whose futures she has been entrusted with. She is a woman who remains dedicated to getting her clients the win inspite of the myriads of problems in her own life. She finds a win where there seems to be none. She is the best with words.
